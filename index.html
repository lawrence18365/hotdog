Common Questions

Hot Dog FAQ

Do you use 100% beef hot dogs?
Yes! We exclusively serve 100% Vienna Beef hot dogs. No fillers, no artificial colors, and no compromise on quality. It's the same legendary beef served in the best Chicago hot dog stands.
Where is Funky Franks located?
We are located at 700 Schelfhout Lane, Suite A, Kimberly, WI 54136. We're right in the heart of the village, serving the entire Fox Valley area including Appleton, Kaukauna, and Little Chute.
Do you offer online ordering for pickup?
Absolutely! You can order directly through our website for fast pickup. Most orders are ready in about 10 minutes. Click any of our "Order Online" buttons to get started.
What are your most popular menu items?
Our #1 seller is the classic Chicago Frank, followed closely by the Cowboy Frank (topped with BBQ and crispy onions) and our signature Loaded Fries. Don't forget to try our Italian Beef sandwich—it's a local favorite!
